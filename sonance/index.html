05. SONANCE

Interactive, 2019

We see light, we hear vibrations, we feel pressure, and we taste and smell chemicals. For most, these ways in which we experience the world are discrete and fixed. But what if we could synaesthetically perceive our reality? Sonance explores the union of sight and sound in an alternate dimension where sound is imagined as a plane of light.
